# Transcript Corrections & Flags

The lecture transcript (`Digestive 2 (1).pdf`) is **machine-generated speech-to-text** and mangles
technical vocabulary, drug names, and a few Latin terms. Below is every meaningful mis-transcription
found, with the correct term. **Trust this list over the raw transcript** when studying.

> Legend: ❌ what the transcript says → ✅ what was actually said/meant.

| **"Iodochloric acid"** | **hydrochloric acid (HCl)** | Repeated near the stomach-histology section |
| **"secrategogue" / "secretogogue" / "secretant dogs"** | **secretagogue** | A trigger for secretion (ACh, gastrin, histamine) — also misspelled on the slide |

| **"sphenosis"** (pyloric) | **stenosis** | Pyloric **stenosis** = a narrowing, common in newborns |
| **"gastroglycose"** | **gastric lipase** | The stomach enzyme that begins fat/lipid breakdown |
| **"domestication"** | **mastication** | Chewing (in the oral cavity) |
| **"muscarinic and cholinergic"** | **muscarinic and nicotinic** | The two acetylcholine-receptor classes (M3 is the parietal-cell type) |
