## Initial finding — three minor gaps, all now remediated

1. **Slide 17 autoclave number.** The deck's overview slide mislabels the autoclave as "100 °C under
   1 atm OR 15 psi." The materials correctly gave 121 °C. **Remediation:** added an explicit
   slide-typo note in the study guide (§6) and the slide-17 gallery caption, telling the student the
   slide says 100 °C but the correct sterilizing value is **121 °C at 15 psi**.
2. **Slide 58 hand-hygiene technique.** The Grimebusters slide's fine print gives ABHR-vs-wash rule
   and timings. **Remediation:** added to study guide §11 and the slide-58 caption — ABHR preferred
   unless visibly soiled (~15–20 s); wash with soap + friction ≥30 s if soiled; remove jewelry.
3. **Slide 63 / Table 9.3 dropped row.** The pore-size table skipped the "3 µm — yeasts and larger
   unicellular algae" row. **Remediation:** row re-inserted into the study-guide table.

No high-importance concept was missing; the two substantive gaps (1, 2) and the trivial one (3) are
all folded into the materials and the app data.
